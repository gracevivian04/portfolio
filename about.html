Hey, I'm Grace!

I'm 31, originally from a small village in the north of England. I'm based in Barcelona after having decided to move here on a whim, post-uni, as a none-the-wiser 22 year old. Safe to say it's been one of the best decisions of my life so far! I have a background in teaching, along with Account Management in a SaaS start-up environment.

I was looking for a career switch once I reached a certain level in the Start-up business I worked for. In my role as a Account Management Team Lead, I had dabbled in data and had been part of 'behind the scenes' decision making that sparked my interest in the tech side of business. I started looking around for opportunities to move into this area and came across a scholorship for a bootcamp. After having completed a bootcamp with KeepCoding a Glovo, I've been looking for the next challenge and opportunity to further my knowledge in this field.
